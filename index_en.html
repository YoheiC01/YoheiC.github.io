Researcher Profile

Self-Introduction

Researching photocatalysts for environmental and energy applications.

Academic Background

  • April 2015 – March 2019: Bachelor’s Program, Department of Inorganic Materials, Tokyo Institute of Technology
  • April 2019 – March 2021: Master’s Program, School of Materials and Chemical Technology, Tokyo Institute of Technology
  • April 2021 – September 2023: Doctoral Program, School of Materials and Chemical Technology, Tokyo Institute of Technology
  • April 2022 – October 2022: Visiting Student, Department of Chemistry, Imperial College London
  • October 2023 – March 2024: JSPS Postdoctoral Fellow, Tokyo Institute of Technology
  • April 2024 – Present: JSPS Postdoctoral Fellow, Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology

Research Areas

Specializing in photocatalysis, focusing on the environmental applications of catalyst materials and the development of efficient solar-driven processes for sustainable energy conversion.
